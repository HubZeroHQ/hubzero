# HubZero Engineering Profiles

This directory contains the source content for HubZero's Engineering Profiles.

Engineering Profiles are not employee biographies or resumes. They are editorial profiles that document how an engineer thinks, what they build, and the philosophy that guides their work.

Each founder profile begins as a Markdown document that is later imported into the HubZero CMS.

---

## Purpose

These documents serve as the canonical source for each Engineering Profile before they are migrated into the CMS.

The website may evolve, but the written content originates here.

## Engineering Profile Philosophy

An Engineering Profile is earned.

It recognizes individuals who have demonstrated sustained technical leadership, meaningful contributions, and embody HubZero's engineering culture.

Engineering Profiles are not automatically granted by employment or title.

Initially, only the founding team holds Engineering Profiles.

Future profiles will be awarded by the Founder Board to engineers who have made exceptional long-term contributions to HubZero.

---

## Permanent Identifier

Every Engineering Profile receives a permanent identifier.

Examples:

- EP-003 — Rifaque Ahmed
- EP-004 — Raif Karani
- EP-005 — Mohammed Iyad
- EP-006 — Syed Mohammed Sultan
- EP-007 — Salsabeel Kobattey

Profile identifiers are never reused.

If a profile is retired, its identifier remains reserved as part of HubZero's engineering history.

---

## Editing Guidelines

Engineering Profiles should be updated rather than rewritten.

Changes should reflect genuine growth, new projects, and evolving engineering perspectives while preserving historical context.

The goal is to create living engineering documents rather than static biographies.

---

## Future Migration

These Markdown files are intended to be imported into the HubZero CMS once Engineering Profiles become a first-class content type.

After migration, the CMS becomes the editing interface while preserving these documents as historical source material.
